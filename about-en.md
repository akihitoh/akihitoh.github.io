## Research Interests
- **Neural-network-based surrogate models for simulation**: policy optimization for pedestrian flow, performance evaluation of autonomous-driving communications, radio propagation maps, coverage estimation for satellite phased arrays
- **Optimization and service development for smart cities**: evacuation route planning (linear programming and Transformer guidance), dispatching for nursing-care taxis, Pub/Sub-based IoT message delivery
- **3D point cloud and UAV-based spatial/human sensing**: tracking and counting of dense crowds, building recognition and anomaly detection from UAV point clouds, person detection under occlusion using mmWave radar
- **Vehicular/traffic sensing and safety support**: smartphone-based detection of risky traffic situations, highway on-ramp merging point prediction, lane-level traffic volume extraction from traffic video
- **Emergency and disaster-resilient networking**: LoRa-based emergency network scheduling, communication and information aggregation platforms for disasters, electronic triage systems
- **Large-scale network analysis and simulation**: delay evaluation of in-vehicle networks based on Network Calculus, wireless interference coordination, scheduling of densely-deployed roadside units and base stations
- **Smart buildings and occupancy-aware energy control**: modeling of daily-living know-how, thermal-comfort-aware HVAC control, task/ambient lighting
- **System design accounting for human mobility**: human/pedestrian tracking, mobility models, crowd sensing
